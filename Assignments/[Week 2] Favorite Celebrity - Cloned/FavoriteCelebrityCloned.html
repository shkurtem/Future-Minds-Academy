Muhammad Al Luhaidan

Sheikh Saleh Muhammad Al-Luhaidan (Arabic: صالح بن محمد اللحيدان; 1932 5 January 2022) was a Saudi scholar, judge, imam, preacher, and member of the Council of Senior Scholars. He was also a teacher of Sharia sciences.



Abdulrahman Mossad

Abdulrahman Mosad is a Quran reciter from Egypt.He is son of Prince Musa'id bin Abdulaziz Al Saud.




Mansour As Salmi

Mansour Al-Salmi, from Saudi Arabia, the city of Jeddah, was born on 6/7/1983 AD. Mansour Al-Salmi became very famous among the community and on all social media platforms, and the number of his fans increased. He is a preacher and reciter of the Holy Quran with humble recitations and a beautiful voice. He even has wonderful chants with a beautiful voice. He won the love of the Islamic and cultural community, as he possesses a wonderful method of persuasion. He also shares his diaries on social media, contributes to charitable projects, and is always present in such projects
